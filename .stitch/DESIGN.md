# Design System: ReservePro
**Project ID:** 2913699036378237577

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
Clean, modern, highly organized, and accessible enterprise style. The atmosphere is professional and productivity-focused, designed with clean whitespace, sharp alignments, and intuitive functional zones inspired by Ant Design v5 specifications.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Primary Blue (#1677ff):** Used for primary buttons, active states, loading indicators, and active sidebar items.
* **Secondary Slate (#595959):** Used for secondary text, labels, and helper captions.
* **Background Light Gray (#f7f9fc):** Used for the overall application background behind cards.
* **Container White (#ffffff):** Used for card backgrounds, sidebar background, and tables.
* **Border Light (#f0f0f0):** Used for dividers and card borders.
* **Success Green (#52c41a):** Used for successful reservation messages.
* **Danger Red (#ff4d4f):** Used for delete actions and input validation errors.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Font Family:** `Inter`, sans-serif.
* **Headers:** Semibold (`font-weight: 600` or `700`) with letter-spacing `-0.02em` for headings to give it a modern and clean look.
* **Body:** Regular (`font-weight: 400`) and Medium (`500`) for standard content.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Standard height of 32px (small), 40px (middle), or 44px (large). Rounded corners (6px). Font weight 600. Primary buttons use a solid blue background (#1677ff) with white text.
* **Cards/Containers:** Background is container white (#ffffff). Thin light border (#f0f0f0). Corner roundness is 8px. Box shadow is very light and diffused (0 2px 8px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)).
* **Inputs/Forms:** 6px rounded corners, 1px border (#d9d9d9), focus state highlights with a 2px blue outline ring (#1677ff).
* **Sidebar Menu Items:** Transition smoothly on hover. Inactive items have a transparent background and dark text. Active items have a blue background (#1677ff) and white text.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Spacing System:** Based on multiples of 8px (8px padding/margin, 16px, 24px, 32px).
* **Grids:** Responsive grid system using Ant Design's `Row` and `Col`. Left-to-right alignment with proper hierarchy.
* **Margins:** Page layouts have 24px padding around content on desktop, and 16px padding on mobile.
